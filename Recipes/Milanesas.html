Milanesas

A piece of steak breaded with various spices

Ingredients

  • Meat of choice
  • Egg
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Parsley
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cut the meat into thin slices
  2. Dump a meat slice into the eggs
  3. Coat the meat with the mixture of breadcrumbs and parsley
  4. Add salt
  5. Deep fry the meat until golden brown
