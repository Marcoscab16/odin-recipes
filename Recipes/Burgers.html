Burgers

A simple burger

Ingredients

  • Grounded beef
  • American cheese
  • Buns
  • BBQ sauce

Steps

  1. Toast the buns with butter
  2. Grab a ball of meat that weights about 130gr
  3. Set it on a hot stove and flatten it with a spatula for 4 minutes
  4. Flip it and add the cheese slice
  5. Add the BBQ sauce to the buns
  6. Put the meat between the buns
