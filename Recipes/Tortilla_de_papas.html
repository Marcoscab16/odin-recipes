Tortilla de papas

A sort of pie made of potatoes and eggs

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Peel and cut the potatoes into thin slices
  2. Fry all the potato slices until they are mushy
  3. Add the potatoes to 3 to 5 beaten eggs
  4. Dump the mixture into a hot oiled pan
  5. Flip it in 2 to 5 minutes, depending on if you want a runny inside or not
